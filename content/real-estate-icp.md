# ICP — Luxury Apartments Medellín (Foreign Buyer + High-End Investor)

## Primary ICPs (ranked)

### ICP A: Lifestyle Upgrade Foreigner (Core)
- **Origin:** US / Canada / Europe (incl. Ireland/UK), occasionally LATAM expats
- **Age:** 30–60
- **Profile:** high-income professional, founder, executive, remote/hybrid
- **Why buy:** quality of life + “base” in LATAM, views, climate, services, security
- **Decision drivers:** trust, simplicity, legality, curated quality, bilingual support
- **Big fears:** scams, unclear title, HOA/building issues, taxes, closing complexity, language barrier
- **Typical budget bands:** define as $X–$Y (USD) or COP; use ranges not exact numbers
- **Qualification signals:**
  - timeline ≤ 3 months
  - can travel soon / already in Colombia
  - budget above minimum
  - cash or pre-approval
  - willing to provide proof-of-funds later

### ICP B: Capital Preservation Investor (High Value)
- **Origin:** Colombian HNW, expats with capital, regional LATAM buyers
- **Profile:** investor optimizing store-of-value + optional rental yield
- **Why buy:** diversification, asset hardening, hedge, prime building scarcity
- **Big fears:** liquidity/resale, admin fees, building restrictions, STR rules, hidden maintenance
- **Qualification signals:**
  - clear target (capital preservation vs yield)
  - cash allocation ready
  - building preference signals (amenities/security/reputation)

### ICP C: Trophy / Ultra-Luxury Buyer (Selective)
- **Origin:** global + local UHNW
- **Profile:** prestige-driven, privacy, uniqueness, “only one like this”
- **Big fears:** time waste, lack of discretion, mediocre service
- **Qualification signals:**
  - concierge expectations
  - confidentiality preference
  - fast decision cycle when fit is perfect

## Exclusions (filter out)
- “Just browsing”
- No defined budget
- Timeline > 12 months with no capital readiness
- Wants luxury but refuses minimum price band
- Unwilling to answer basic qualification questions

## ICP Messaging Anchors
- **Curation:** “Only trophy-grade buildings and verified listings.”
- **Safety:** “Foreign-buyer safe process: due diligence, taxes, closing support.”
- **Speed:** “Shortlist in 24–48h based on your criteria.”
- **Discretion:** “Private tours & confidential handling for premium clients.”
