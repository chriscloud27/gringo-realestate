## 7) Brand System Specs (Black + Gold)
### Colors (baseline — adjust to the client’s exact gold if known)
- Obsidian (bg): `#0B0B0F`
- Charcoal (surface): `#12121A`
- Antique Gold (accent): `#C8A24A`
- Champagne (highlight): `#E3C77A`
- Off-white (text): `#F3F0EA`
- Muted gray (secondary text): `#B9B6B0`

**Gold usage rule:** ≤ 8% of any screen; used for lines, icons, borders, hover states, small emphasis.

### Typography (timeless luxury)
- Headlines: editorial serif (e.g., Canela / Cormorant Garamond / Playfair Display)
- Body: modern sans (e.g., Inter / Helvetica Now style)
- Labels: subtle small caps (sparingly)

### Imagery rules
- Cinematic architecture, clean compositions, high dynamic range but natural
- Mix: hero wide view + interior geometry + materials detail + amenities (only premium)
- Avoid: clutter, heavy filters, amateur angles

## 8) Logo suggestion (timeless + outstanding)
**Recommended:** Monogram + Wordmark system
- Monogram: brand initials in an engraved serif style, symmetrical, high legibility at small sizes.
- Optional thin “frame” (square or minimal shield outline) to echo the “gallery” concept.
- Wordmark: spaced serif, with a small caps descriptor:
  - “LUXURY REAL ESTATE”
  - “MEDELLÍN | PRIVATE CLIENTS”

**Required lockups:**
- Full: monogram + wordmark + descriptor
- Wordmark only
- Monogram only (favicon/social)
- Single-color: gold-on-black and black-on-white
