# Website Sections (Luxury Medellín) — What You Need + Why

## Homepage (conversion-focused order)

### 1) Hero (above fold)
- Value proposition (luxury + foreign-buyer safety)
- Primary CTA: **Request curated shortlist**
- Secondary CTA: **View featured properties**
- Soft qualifier: “Luxury inventory starts from $X” (optional)

### 2) Featured Portfolio (trophy case)
- 6–12 max
- Big visuals + 3 key facts + price band
- CTA on each: “Request details”

### 3) Trust Strip (fast proof)
- Years in market
- Bilingual advisory
- Due diligence included
- Partner network
- Curated inventory only

### 4) Signature Process (foreigners)
- 5–7 steps with simple icons
- Mention due diligence + closing support + timeline expectations
- CTA: “Apply to start”

### 5) Social Proof
- Testimonials (prefer foreigners)
- Mini case studies (profile → goal → result)
- Partner logos (law/tax/relocation)

### 6) About (agent credibility)
- Portrait + short story + values
- Specialization: luxury buildings/neighborhoods
- CTA: “Work with me (apply)”

### 7) Buyer Resources (education = trust + SEO)
- “How foreigners buy in Colombia”
- Taxes, closing costs, timelines
- Best neighborhoods comparisons
- CTA: “Get the guide” or “Apply for shortlist”

### 8) Final CTA + Contact
- Two paths:
  - Apply (high intent)
  - WhatsApp (lower friction, but after basic filters)

## Listings Page
- Filterable grid: budget, neighborhood, beds, features
- Tags: “Foreign-buyer friendly”, “Verified building”
- Sticky CTA: “Request shortlist”

## Listing Detail Page
1) Hero image/video + quick facts
2) Price / band + admin fees (if available)
3) “Why this property” bullets
4) Full gallery + floor plan (if available)
5) Neighborhood & building amenities
6) Due diligence notes (what’s already verified)
7) Sticky CTA: “Request details / schedule viewing”

## About Page
- Story, credentials, approach
- Partner network
- Testimonials
- CTA: Apply

## Contact / Apply Page (gated)
- Multi-step qualification form + scheduling only for qualified
