# How to Target / Market / Sell (Luxury Medellín) — High-Intent Playbook

## Principle
Stop “talking to everyone.” Build a funnel that:
1) attracts high-intent buyers,
2) proves credibility fast,
3) forces qualification before calls,
4) moves qualified leads into viewings.

## Acquisition Channels (highest quality first)

### 1) Google Search (intent capture)
- Targets: “buy apartment Medellín”, “penthouses El Poblado”, “luxury apartment Medellín”, “buy property Colombia foreigner”
- Assets needed:
  - SEO landing pages for neighborhoods
  - “Foreign buyers” guide
  - Listings pages with filters
- Conversion: “Request curated shortlist” → qualification form

### 2) Instagram + YouTube (proof engine)
- Content that converts:
  - cinematic walkthroughs (30–90s)
  - “how foreigners buy safely” explainers
  - “best buildings in [neighborhood]” short guides
- CTA always: “Apply for shortlist” (not “DM me for info”)

### 3) Retargeting (only warm traffic)
- Show ads only to:
  - people who viewed ≥2 listings
  - people who visited foreign-buyer page
  - people who started form
- Goal: push them into qualification, not into chat

### 4) Partnerships (silent killer channel)
- Partners:
  - immigration lawyers
  - tax advisors
  - relocation firms
  - private bankers / wealth managers
  - premium property managers
- Offer:
  - co-branded guide
  - referral fee where legal
  - VIP shortlist service

## Sales System (pipeline)
- Stage 1: Inquiry → (auto) lead score
- Stage 2: Qualified → shortlist sent + call scheduled
- Stage 3: Viewing → offer strategy
- Stage 4: Due diligence → title/HOA/building/taxes
- Stage 5: Close → aftercare + referrals

## Conversion Rules (anti-time-waster)
- Show “from” price or price band.
- Use minimum budget gate before scheduling.
- Offer “VIP shortlist” (scarcity) instead of “browse everything.”
- Use “2 paths”:
  - **High intent:** Apply + schedule
  - **Low intent:** Download guide (email capture) + nurture

## Follow-up Standards (speed matters)
- Lead score 80–100: contact in < 30 minutes (WhatsApp + email)
- 60–79: contact within 24 hours
- <60: nurture sequence only until re-qualified
