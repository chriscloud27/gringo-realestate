# Customer Profiling Process — Qualification Form + Lead Scoring + Routing

## Goal
Reduce tire-kickers, capture high-intent buyer data, score leads, and route to the right next step automatically.

## Step 1: Trigger Points (where the form appears)
- “Request curated shortlist”
- “Request full details”
- “Schedule private viewing”
- “Download property dossier”

## Step 2: Multi-step Form Questions (high signal)
### Contact
1) Full name
2) Email
3) WhatsApp

### Intent & Readiness
4) Are you currently in Colombia? (Yes/No)
5) When do you plan to buy? (0–30d / 1–3m / 3–6m / 6m+)
6) Buyer type (Cash / Financing / Investor / Relocating)
7) Budget range (dropdown; enforce minimum)
8) Preferred neighborhoods (multi-select)
9) Must-haves (checkboxes: view, terrace, security, amenities, parking, etc.)
10) Deal blockers (open text): “Anything that could delay your purchase?”
11) Optional: Proof of funds / bank letter upload OR checkbox: “I can provide upon request”

## Step 3: Lead Scoring (0–100)
- Budget meets minimum: +25
- Budget high tier (top band): +10
- Timeline ≤ 3 months: +20
- In Colombia now OR visiting within 30 days: +15
- Cash buyer: +15
- Proof of funds provided: +25
- Clear criteria (neighborhood + must-haves filled): +5

## Step 4: Automatic Routing Rules
- Score 80–100 (Hot):
  - unlock scheduling link immediately
  - notify agent via WhatsApp + email with summary
  - create CRM card in “Qualified”
- Score 60–79 (Warm):
  - show “We’ll send a shortlist within 48h”
  - agent follow-up within 24h
  - CRM card in “New”
- Score <60 (Cold):
  - no call offered
  - deliver guide + limited shortlist
  - nurture sequence until they re-qualify

## Step 5: Auto-Responses
- Email/WhatsApp confirmation:
  - “Received. Here’s next steps.”
  - link to buyer guide
  - expected response time
- Optional: request documents later (for Hot leads only)

## Step 6: CRM Fields (minimum)
- Name, email, WhatsApp
- Budget band
- Timeline
- Buyer type
- In-country status
- Preferred neighborhoods
- Lead score
- Source (Google/IG/Referral/Retargeting)
- Notes + next action date

## Step 7: Agent Script (micro)
- Open with their criteria + confirm budget/timeline
- Move to: shortlist + scheduling + due diligence overview
- Close with: next step date/time + what they need to bring (if any)
