The Story

I came to Medellín the way most of my clients do. Drawn by the climate, the energy, and a quality of life that simply doesn't exist at the same price point anywhere else in the world. I came for a short stay. I stayed for five years and counting.

In those first years I learned the market from the inside. As a buyer, as a tenant, and eventually as someone who started helping other English-speaking foreigners navigate the same process I'd stumbled through myself. What I found was a consistent gap: plenty of local agents, almost none with the combination of English fluency, genuine market knowledge, and a network strong enough to get things done properly.

I built Luxury Apartments Medellín to fill that gap. Today our practice covers everything a foreign buyer needs under one roof: luxury apartments, commercial leases, property sales, lease-to-own financing, escrow title services, and power of attorney for clients who need a trusted local representative.

The English-speaking professional network I've built in this city is one of our strongest assets — it means faster due diligence, better legal briefings, and off-market access that most buyers never see.

The Standard

What Every Client
Can Expect.

These are not aspirations. They are the minimum standard that every engagement is held to — because the alternative is exactly the market problem we exist to solve.

Standard 01
I only show what I would buy myself.

Every property in our portfolio has been physically visited and personally assessed before any client sees it. We reject approximately 80% of properties we view — because presenting something mediocre wastes everyone's time and tells you nothing about our judgement.

Standard 02
Due diligence is never optional.

Title history, HOA financial health, building permits, developer track record, strata classification, litigation status — these checks happen before you see a property. Not after. The building you buy needs to be as sound as the apartment you're walking through.

Transparency over commission.
Standard 03

When there is a reason to hesitate on a property, you will hear it from me first. Our long-term business depends entirely on clients who got exactly what they expected — or better. A commission earned on a bad transaction costs far more than it is worth.

Speed matters as much as quality.
Standard 04

Good properties in Medellín move fast. Qualified buyers who have their documentation ready, their criteria clear, and a trusted advisor who can act — those are the buyers who close. Part of my job is making sure you are positioned to move when the right thing appears.

Discretion is a standard, not a feature.
Standard 05

Premium transactions involve sensitive financial information and personal circumstances. Confidentiality is absolute. Off-market properties are presented privately. Negotiations are conducted without unnecessary parties. Your business remains your business.

The relationship continues after closing.
Standard 06

The closing is not the end of the engagement — it is the beginning of a longer relationship. Property management referrals, STR setup, lease management, future resale strategy — all of this is available through the same network that handled your purchase.
